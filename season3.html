The third season of Adventure Time premiered on Cartoon Network from July 11, 2011 to February 13, 2012 (US), and contains a total of twenty six 11-minute episodes. Season 3 of Adventure Time premiered on July 11, 2011 with the episode "Conquest of Cuteness", which was watched by 2.686 million viewers in the US, which is higher than Adventure Time's previous season premieres. The episode with the highest number of viewers was "Fionna and Cake, which had a total of 3.32 million viewers. Throughout the season most episodes had around 2 million viewers at premiere.

Major characters

Ice king

Ice king is originally depicted as a two-dimensional, humorously over-the-top villain trying to kidnap Princess Bubblegum and various other Princesses on a regular basis, the character's personality was subsequently expanded, revealing him to be a kind-hearted but misunderstood old man with completely twisted senses of morality and social behavior, suffering from loneliness and memory loss.

Finn

Finn is a teenage human, resembling one in most of his appearances throughout the series. He was roughly five feet at the show's debut, but sometime between "The Cooler" and "The Pajama War," he underwent a growth spurt and is currently nearly as tall as Princess Bubblegum.

Jake

Jake is a magical dog and Finn's constant companion, best friend, and adoptive brother. Jake has Stretchy Powers, which allow him to stretch and manipulate his body, coming in handy on innumerable occasions throughout his and Finn's adventures.

Minor characters

Breakfast Princess

Breakfast Princess is the princess of Breakfast Kingdom. She has egg-white hair and egg-yolk skin. She has bacon on her head, which appears to function as a crown, and a top consisting of buttered toast, and a bacon belt. Her skirt looks like a pancake with syrup on it, while her shoes resemble cracked eggshells. Breakfast Princess's clothing is completely edible, as shown by Lumpy Space Princess eating them when she was pranking her with Marceline.

Toast Princess

Toast Princess is a sentient piece of toasted bread. She wears a long, red sleeveless dress with a pink collar and buttons, as well as a toasted waffle headband (which seems to act as her crown and signify her royalty). She also has a cooked egg on top of the left side of her head.

Snail

The Snail is a character that was hidden in every episode of Adventure Time to date Adventure Time (Pilot), usually seen waving at the screen. His cameos are a running gag. In the episode "Mortal Folly," The Lich possesses the Snail to break the amber prison where he is trapped. Then, in "Mortal Recoil," Princess Bubblegum (while being possessed), passes the Lich onto him.
