The first season of Adventure Time premiered on Cartoon Network from April 5, 2010 to September 27, 2010 (US), and contains a total of twenty six 11-minute episodes.The first two episodes of Adventure Time, "Slumber Party Panic" and "Trouble in Lumpy Space", both premiered on Cartoon Network on Monday April 5, 2010 at 8:00 pm with 2.5 million US viewers, which is the highest number of views for the whole season. The rest of the season premiered with between 1.5 million and 2.5 million US viewers.

Major characters

Finn

Finn is a teenage human, resembling one in most of his appearances throughout the series. He was roughly five feet at the show's debut, but sometime between "The Cooler" and "The Pajama War," he underwent a growth spurt and is currently nearly as tall as Princess Bubblegum.

Jake

Jake is a magical dog and Finn's constant companion, best friend, and adoptive brother. Jake has Stretchy Powers, which allow him to stretch and manipulate his body, coming in handy on innumerable occasions throughout his and Finn's adventures.

Princess Bubblegum

Princess Bubblegum is a main character of Adventure Time. She made her first appearance in the animated short "Adventure Time!." She is the ruler of the Candy Kingdom. Princess Bubblegum is Finn's former love intrest . She is Ice King's most targeted Princess. She is 18 years old.

Minor characters

Shoko

Shoko, also called the "Ghost Lady", is an undead character in Adventure Time. She was once a thief who is assigned by the Bath Boy Gang to steal the teen Princess Bubblegum's amulet but died to fall in a river while attempting to steal it in the night. Shoko then became a ghost known by Finn as "The Ghost Lady" and first debut in "The Creeps".

Minerva Kampbell

Minerva Kampbell is a doctor, the only surviving Helper after an epidemic of an accidentally released biological virus by Dr. Gross, and Finn's biological mother. Because she wired her mind into the computer system and sent it to hundreds of robot clones during the worst biological virus epidemic of the past 300 years, which caused many deaths, some survived, spawning the next generations of humans on Founder's Island.

Elise

Elise is a tall, thin woman with a dark complexion and short, shaggy black hair. She has the usual skinny arms and legs of most humanoid characters, and in her later years, she would also show slight bags underneath her eyes. She wears a green long-sleeved jacket, a white shirt, blue jeans with holes in the knees, white socks, and dark-green high-top sneakers. During a flashback in "Obsidian," she is also seen wearing a red cloak.
